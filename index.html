Personal Projects

EthTracker

A personal website I developed to dynamically track the price of Ethereum. It features a real-time stream of price/volume Ethereum data, an interactive chart that plots closing price vs time, and an interactive timeline chart with a few notable events in the development of Ethereum. Link to website.

EthWidget

An Android widget I developed using Android Studio to provide real time updates on the price of Ethereum as well as the 24 hour percentage change. With a minimalistic design, it updates periodically at 1 hour intervals and can be manually updated with the refresh button.
